I am Postdoctoral Fellow in Survey Research at the Blavatnik School of Government, University of Oxford. I received my PhD from the Department of Political Science at Washington University in St. Louis. My research interests are in comparative party politics and political behavior, with a focus on party campaigns, party rhetoric, voter behavior, and voter attitudes in advanced democracies.

Before graduate school, I studied at Underwood International College of Yonsei University in Seoul, South Korea. I received a BA in Political Science and International Relations with highest honors.
